## Help for general issues


* Please check to see if your question is already answered on this wiki.

* Please contact the KIR Resarch Computing Manager, Dini Senanayake who can be contacted on Teams or by email (kir-rc@kennedy.ox.ac.uk OR dini.senanayake@kennedy.ox.ac.uk).

* There is a central BMRC team which provides assistance for all BMRC users. They can be contacted at bmrc-help@medsci.ox.ac.uk for general support enquiries.

* You can ask other KIR users for assistance in the "BMRC help" channel on the Microsoft Teams [KIR Computational Biology Team](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3A4c4e403abe604ec2bb5a16917ed080a2%40thread.tacv2/BMRC%20help?groupId=13eeb4db-5544-4d79-8ffa-102a97159e00&tenantId=cc95de1b-97f5-4f93-b4ba-fe68b852cf91). Experienced KIR users are monitoring this channel and will help if they can.

## Working with restricted or identifiable datasets

If you need to work with datasets that are restricted by access agreements or CDA/NDA or which contain identifiable or pseudoanonymised human data please contact dini.senanayake@kennedy.ox.ac.uk with stephen.sansom@kennedy.ac.uk in cc.
